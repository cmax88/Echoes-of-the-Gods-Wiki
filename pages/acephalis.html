Acephalis


Description

The Acephalis are a race of humanoids with the head of a horse instead of a human head. They are known for their speed, agility, and their strong sense of community. They are revered by many as symbols of freedom and independence, and they are respected for their strong connection to nature.

Acephalis are highly agile and athletic, and they are able to move with incredible speed. They are also highly intuitive and empathetic, and they are able to sense the emotions of others with ease. They are very social creatures, and they form close-knit communities that are based on mutual respect and support.

Acephalis are skilled in a variety of activities, including hunting, tracking, and horseback riding. They are also highly skilled in combat, and they are able to use their speed and agility to defeat their enemies. They are also able to use their intuition and empathy to communicate with animals and to understand their emotions.

Despite their horse-like appearance, Acephalis are fully sentient beings, and they have a rich culture and history. They are deeply connected to the natural world, and they believe that all creatures should be treated with respect and dignity. They are highly respected for their wisdom and their deep connection to the natural world, and they are often sought after for their advice and guidance.

Traits:

  • Loyal Companions: The Acephalis are fiercely loyal to their friends and families and will defend them to the death if necessary. They have a +2 bonus to Strength.
  • Trance: Like elves, Acephalis don't need to sleep. Instead, they meditate deeply, remaining semiconscious, for 4 hours a day. After resting in this way, you gain the same benefits that other races do from 8 hours of sleep.
  • Equine Physiology: The Acephalis have the head of a horse and a horse-like body, giving them exceptional speed and agility. They have a +2 bonus to Dexterity and a +1 bonus to Speed.
  • Darkvision: You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can't discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.
  • Social Creatures: The Acephalis are a highly social race, living in large herds and communities. They have a +2 bonus to Charisma.

  • Base Speed: 30ft
  • Size: 6-8ft; Medium
  • Languages: Common, Elvish, and Sylvan
