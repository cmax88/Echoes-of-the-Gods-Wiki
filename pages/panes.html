Panes


Description

The Panes are a mysterious and reclusive race of half-man, half-goat creatures who are known for their cunning and their skill in magic. They are highly sought after by many adventurers, as they are known for their knowledge of the ancient and powerful magic of the world.

Panes have the upper body of a man and the lower body of a goat, and they are short and nimble. They have sharp, angular features and their eyes are bright and full of mischief. They are covered in shaggy fur, and their goat-like legs allow them to move quickly and nimbly.

Panes are skilled in magic, and they are known for their knowledge of ancient spells and incantations. They are also skilled in illusions and trickery, and they are able to use their magic to deceive and mislead their enemies.

Despite their cunning and their skill, Panes are also known for their kindness and their compassion. They are deeply connected to nature and the creatures of the land, and they are known for their love of music and their joyous celebrations.

The Panes are a mysterious and reclusive race, and they are often misunderstood by those who do not know them. Despite this, they are a force for good in the world, and they will stop at nothing to protect their lands and their people.

Traits:

  • Illusion: Panes are skilled in illusions and trickery, and they have a +2 bonus to Wisdom. They are able to use their magic to deceive and mislead their enemies, and to protect themselves and their allies.
    • Magic Resistance: You have advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.
  • Music: Panes are known for their love of music, and they have a +2 bonus to Charisma. They are able to use their music to inspire and motivate their allies.
  • Joy: Panes are known for their joy and their celebration, and they have a +2 bonus to Intelligence. They are able to use their joy and their positive energy to influence and persuade others, and to bring happiness and light into the world.
    • Mirthful Leaps: Whenever you make a long or high jump, you can roll a d12 and add the number to the number of feet you cover, even when making a standing jump. This extra distance costs movement as normal.
  • Base Speed: 45ft
  • Size: 4-5ft; Medium
  • Languages: Common, Sylvan
