Atlantians


Description

The Atlantians are a powerful and ancient race that hails from the sunken city of Atlantis. They are a proud and regal people, known for their mastery of magic and their advanced technology.

Atlantians are tall and elegant, with pale skin and hair that ranges from silver to gold. They have piercing blue eyes that seem to glow with an inner light, and they possess otherworldly grace and beauty.

Atlantians are a highly advanced race, with a deep understanding of magic and technology. They are known for their mastery of elemental magic, and they are able to control the forces of nature with ease. They are also skilled in the creation of magical artifacts and devices, and they possess advanced technology that is far beyond the understanding of other races.

In combat, Atlantians are powerful and skilled. They have a deep understanding of magic, and they are able to use their powers to control the elements and summon powerful creatures to aid them in battle. They are also skilled in the use of advanced weapons and armor, and they are not afraid to use their technology to gain an advantage in combat.

Despite their power and sophistication, Atlantians are also known for their arrogance and their disdain for other races. They believe themselves to be superior to all other races, and they are often seen as aloof and distant. However, they are also fiercely loyal to their own kind, and they will stop at nothing to protect their city and their people.

The Atlantians are a mysterious and enigmatic race, and their true motivations and goals are often shrouded in mystery. They are feared by many, and their power and technology make them a force to be reckoned with in any conflict.

Traits:

  • Arrogance: Atlantians are known for their arrogance and their disdain for other races. They have a +2 bonus to Charisma and can use their charisma to intimidate and manipulate others.
  • Intuition: Atlantians have a deep intuition and a strong connection to the magic of the world. They have a +2 bonus to Wisdom and can sense when something is wrong or when danger is near.
    • Marine Recovery: If you complete a short rest while fully submerged in water throughout, you can regain extra hit points equal to your 10 times your level.
  • Advanced Technology: Atlantians possess advanced technology that is far beyond the understanding of other races. They have a +2 bonus to Intelligence and can use their technology to gain an advantage in combat.
    • Atlantean Weapon Training: You have proficiency with the spear, trident, javelin, and net.
  • Damage Resistance: Cold
  • Base Speed: 30ft, swim 35ft
  • Size: 5-7ft; Medium
  • Languages: Aquan, Common
