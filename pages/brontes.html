Brontes


Description

The Brontes are a race of human-sized cyclops, known for their immense strength and intimidating appearance. They are large creatures, standing at around eight to ten feet tall, with broad shoulders and a muscular build. Their most distinctive feature is their single eye, which is large and glowing, set in the center of their forehead.

Despite their fearsome appearance, the Brontes are a peaceful and solitary race. They live in remote caves and forests, away from the hustle and bustle of civilization. They are skilled craftsmen and blacksmiths, known for their ability to forge powerful weapons and armor from the materials they find in the wilderness.

The Brontes are shy and reserved, preferring to keep to themselves and avoid contact with other races. However, they are fiercely protective of their homes and will defend themselves if they feel threatened. They are also known to be wise and knowledgeable, often possessing a deep understanding of nature and the world around them.

In combat, the Brontes are formidable opponents. Their single eye gives them excellent aim, and they wield massive hammers and mauls with ease. They are slow and lumbering, but their massive size and strength make them nearly impossible to bring down.

Despite their intimidating appearance, the Brontes are not inherently evil. They are simply a misunderstood race, often feared by others because of their appearance and reputation. Those who take the time to get to know the Brontes may find that they are gentle giants, with a deep love for nature and a wealth of knowledge to share.

Traits:

  • Strength: Brontes are incredibly strong and physically imposing. They have a +2 bonus to Strength and can wield heavy weapons and armor with ease.
    • Up to Standard: You have harnessed the ability to grow to the size of an ancient cyclops. You can expand an action to increases your size to Large for 1 minute. For the duration, your size is doubled in all dimensions, and your weight is multiplied by eight. If there isn’t enough room for you to double your size, you attain the maximum possible size in the space available. For the rest of the duration, you also have advantage on Strength checks and Strength saving throws. Your weapons also grow to match your new size. While these weapons are enlarged, your attacks with them deal 1d4 extra damage. Once you use this ability, you cannot do so again until you finish a long rest.
  • Natural Craftsmen: Brontes are skilled craftsmen, able to forge powerful weapons and armor from the materials they find in the wild. They have a +2 bonus to Constitution.
    • Born for Creation: Your ancestry makes you wired to make things. You gain proficiency in an artisan tool of your choice.
  • Slow and Steady: Brontes are slow and lumbering, but their massive size and strength make them nearly impossible to bring down. They have a +2 bonus to Dexterity and are resistant to knockdown effects.
  • Base Speed: 25ft
  • Size: 8-10ft; Medium
  • Languages: Common, Giant
