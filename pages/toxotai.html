Toxotai Class


Description

The Toxotai class is a group of warriors who specialize in the use of a unique weapon called the toxotis, a special type of bow and arrow. Toxotai are known for their exceptional aim and dexterity, making them formidable foes in battle. These warriors are often sought after by armies and kingdoms, as they can turn the tide in any battle with their exceptional archery skills.

Hit Points:

  • Hit Die: 1d10 per Toxotai Level
  • Hit Points at 1st Level: 10 + Constitution Modifier
  • Hit Points at Higher Levels: 1d10 + Constitution Modifier per Toxotai Level after 1st

Proficiencies:

  • Armor: Light Armor, Medium Armor, Shields
  • Weapons: Simple Weapons, Ranged
  • Tools: None
  • Saving Throws: Strength, Dexterity
  • Skills: Choose two: Animal Handling, Athletics, Insight, Investigation, Nature, Perception, Stealth, and Survival

Starting Equipment:

 

 

Lvl 1: Initiate of the Toxotai

  • Archery Proficiency (Improvisation): The Toxotai class gains proficiency in all ranged weapons, including the bow and arrow.
  • Hunter’s Mark (Bonus Action): The Toxotai class can mark a target as their prey, giving them advantage on all attacks against that target. This effect lasts until the target is defeated or the Toxotai chooses a new target. Can be used 5 times per short rest.

Lvl 2: Swift Archer

  • Toxotis (Weapon): You gain access to the Toxotis, a special bow that deals 1d4 damage and can fire up to 3 arrows at once. Range 50ft. +5 to hit. Unlimited ammunition. Toxotis arrows up 1d4 per Toxotai level.

Lvl 3: Master of the Toxotis

  • Aimed Shot (Passive): You can choose to focus on one target when making an attack roll, adding +2 to your attack roll and an additional 1d6 damage to the attack.
  • Stealth Expertise (Improvisation): The Toxotai class gains advantage in Stealth, allowing them to move unseen through the battlefield.

Lvl 4: Speed of the Arrow

  • Sniper Shot (Bonus Action): The Toxotai class can take a shot from a longer distance (+50ft), dealing extra damage with their ranged weapons (+1d12). Can be used once per short rest.
  • Swift Movement (Improvisation): Your base walking speed increases by 15ft.

Lvl 5: Earth Walker

  • Pinning Shot (Bonus Action): The Toxotai can use their shot to pin a target to the ground, reducing their movement speed by half. Can be used 3 times per long rest.
  • Poison Explosion (Action): The Toxotai can create a cloud of poisonous gas that deals 3d6 poison damage to all creatures within a 15-foot radius. Can be used once per short rest.

Lvl 6: Hunter’s Mark

  • Improved Hunter’s Mark (Improvisation): The Toxotai can now mark three targets at once, giving them advantage on all attacks against those targets.
  • Piercing Shot (Action): The Toxotis’ arrows are capable of piercing through armor and obstacles with ease. They can ignore cover and walls and deal +1d12 damage on a hit. Can be used 3 times per short rest.

Lvl 7: Master of the Hunt

  • Swift Stealth (Bonus Action): The Toxotai can use their bonus action to become invisible for 1 minute. Invisibility is broken once an action is used. Can be used 5 times per long rest.
  • Improved Swift Movement (Improvisation): Your walking speed increases by 20ft.

Lvl 8: Fury of the Toxotis

  • Fury of the Toxotis (Bonus Action): When you make an attack roll, you can choose to deal an additional 5d8 damage per arrow to the target. Can be used once per long rest.

Lvl 9: Hunter of Mortals

  • Poisonous Barrage (Action): The Toxotai can fire a volley of arrows at their enemies, dealing 10d6 poison damage to all enemies within a 20-foot radius. Can be used once per day.

Lvl 10: God of the Hunt

  • Storm of Arrows (Action): The Toxotai gains the ultimate power of the Toxotis’, becoming a true master of archery. They unlock the ability to use their bow to create a massive storm of arrows that can rain down upon their enemies, dealing massive damage and covering a wide area. Can be used once per day and requires a full action to activate. When activated, the Toxotai unleashes a barrage of arrows that deal 10d12 damage to all enemies within a 60-foot radius. The damage dealt by the arrows is considered magical, and enemies who are hit by the arrows are also stunned for 1 round. This ability bypasses all forms of damage reduction, making it a formidable tool for the Toxotai to use in combat.
