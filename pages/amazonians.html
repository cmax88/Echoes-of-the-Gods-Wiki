Amazonians


Description

The Amazonians are a proud and fierce race of warrior women who hail from the lush forests of the Amazon. They are known for their skill in battle and their unwavering loyalty to their tribe and their queen.

Amazonians are tall and muscular, with long hair that is often braided and adorned with feathers and beads. They have sharp, angular features and piercing eyes that seem to see straight through to the soul.

Amazonians are skilled in the use of weapons and armor, and they are known for their mastery of the bow and arrow. They are also skilled in hand-to-hand combat, and they are not afraid to engage in close combat with their enemies.

In battle, Amazonians are relentless and fierce. They are not afraid to charge into the fray, and they will stop at nothing to protect their tribe and their land. They are also known for their cunning and their ability to quickly assess a situation and determine the best course of action.

Despite their ferocity in battle, Amazonians are also known for their compassion and their love of nature. They live in harmony with the forests and the animals that call them home, and they are deeply connected to the magic of the land.

The Amazonians are a proud and noble race, and they are respected by all who know them. They are a force to be reckoned with in any conflict, and they will stop at nothing to protect their tribe and their land.

Traits:

  • Archery Mastery: Amazonians are known for their mastery of the bow and arrow. They have a +2 bonus to Dexterity and can use their archery skills to take down their enemies from a distance.
  • Amazon Weapon Training: You have proficiency with spears, glaives, short bows, longbows, and shields.
  • Nature Connection: Amazonians are deeply connected to the magic of nature and the forests that they call home. They have a +2 bonus to Intelligence and can use their connection to nature to heal themselves and their allies.
  • Earthborn: Amazonians are said to be as strong and as resilient as the earth. Your hit point maximum increases by 5, and it increases by 5 every time you gain a level.
  • Intuition: Amazonians have a deep intuition and a strong connection to the magic of the world. They have a +2 bonus to Wisdom and can sense when something is wrong or when danger is near.

  • Base Speed: 35ft
  • Size: 7-9ft; Medium
  • Languages: Common, Giant
