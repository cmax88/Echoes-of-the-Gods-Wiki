Oracle Class


Description

The Oracle is a divine class that uses their visions and divine insights to guide their allies and defeat their enemies. They have a deep connection with the gods, and they can channel their power to heal their allies, predict the future, and unleash devastating attacks.

Hit Points

  • Hit Die: 1d8 per Oracle Level
  • Hit Points at 1st Level: 8 + Constitution Modifier
  • Hit Points at Higher Levels: 1d8 + Constitution Modifier per Oracle Level after 1st

Proficiencies

  • Armor: None
  • Weapons: Simple Weapons, Shortswords
  • Tools: Choose one:
  • Saving Throws: Strength, Dexterity
  • Skills: Choose two: Acrobatics, Athletics, History, Insight, Religion, and Stealth

Starting Equipment


Lvl 1: Seeker of Wisdom

  • Cure Wounds (Bonus Action): The Oracle can channel their divine power to heal their allies, restoring 1d6 hit points to a single target. Range 10ft. Cantrip.
  • Divine Guidance (Bonus Action): The Oracle can use their visions to guide their allies, giving them a +2 bonus to their next attack roll or saving throw. Cantrip.

Lvl 2: Prophetic Novice

  • Smite Evil (Bonus Action): The Oracle can channel their divine power to smite their enemies, dealing an extra 2d6 damage to a single target. Cantrip. Can be used as a reaction.

Lvl 3: Diviner of Truths

  • Flames of the Prophet (Action): The Oracle can channel their divine power to unleash a barrage of fire, dealing 3d6 damage to all enemies in a 30-foot radius. Cantrip.
  • Divination (Bonus Action): The Oracle can use their visions to predict the outcome of a battle, giving their allies a +3 bonus to their next attack roll. Can be used 5 times per short rest.

Lvl 4: Visionary Sage

  • Mass Cure Wounds (Action): The Oracle can channel their divine power to heal their allies, restoring 3d6 hit points to all allies within 30 feet. Can be used 5 times per long rest.

Lvl 5: Clairvoyant Advisor

  • Holy Strike (Bonus Action): The Oracle can channel their divine power to smite their enemies, dealing an extra 4d6 damage to all enemies. 30ft radius. Cantrip.
  • Vision of the Future (Passive): The Oracle can use their visions to predict the future, giving their allies a +4 bonus to Initiative rolls.

Lvl 6: Unveiler of Prophecies

  • Greater Cure Wounds (Bonus Action): The Oracle can channel their divine power to heal their allies, restoring 5d6 hit points to a single target. Range 10ft. Cantrip.

Lvl 7: Forteller of Fates

  • Righteous Fury (Action): The Oracle can channel their divine power to unleash a barrage of divine energy, dealing 5d6 damage to all enemies in a 30-foot radius. Cantrip.

Lvl 8: Oracle of Omen

  • Curse of the Gods (Bonus Action): The Oracle can call upon the gods to curse their enemies, lowering their defenses and causing them to suffer from ill luck. Can select 3 enemies at once. If cursed, enemies gain all mental and physical effects at once. Enemies must succeed in a DC 16 Constitution saving throw for the spell to have no effect. Can be used once per short rest.

Lvl 9: Divine Interpereter

  • Miraculous Intervention (Action): The Oracle can call upon their deity to intervene in a dire situation, granting them temporary invincibility and allowing them to escape harm. Last 15 turns. During this time, the Oracle can still use all actions and has a 50% increase in attack damage.

Lvl 10: God of Knowledge

  • Divine Revelation (Action): Once per day, the Oracle can call upon their divine patron for guidance and knowledge. For a period of 10 minutes, the Oracle gains an immense insight into the world around them, gaining a +10 bonus to all Knowledge checks, and a +10 bonus to all Perception checks.
    and/or
    Once per day, the Oracle can call upon their divine patron to aid them in combat. For the next 10 rounds, the Oracle gains a +10 bonus to all attack rolls, a +8d6 bonus to all attacks, and a +5 bonus to all saves. During this time, the Oracle is also immune to fear effects and can ignore all forms of physical and mental impairment. The Oracle's divine patron also protects them, granting them temporary hit points equal to twice their level
