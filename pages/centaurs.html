Centaurs


Description

The Centaurs are a proud and noble race of half-human, half-horse creatures who are known for their strength and their courage. They are highly respected by all who know them, and they are known for their wisdom and their skill in battle.

Centaurs have the upper body of a human and the lower body of a horse, and they are tall and muscular. They have long, flowing hair and sharp, angular features, and their eyes are bright and full of intelligence.

Centaurs are skilled in combat, and they are known for their bravery and their unwavering loyalty. They are also skilled in hunting and tracking, and they are able to navigate even the most treacherous terrain with ease.

In battle, Centaurs are fierce and relentless, and they are able to charge into battle with great speed and power. They are also skilled in using their weapons and their magic, and they are able to defend their allies and their lands with great courage and skill.

Despite their strength and their skill, Centaurs are also known for their wisdom and their compassion. They live in harmony with the animals and the plants of the land, and they are deeply connected to the magic of nature.

The Centaurs are a proud and noble race, and they are respected by all who know them. They are a force for good in the world, and they will stop at nothing to protect their lands and their people.

Traits:

  • Strength: Centaurs are known for their strength and their power, and they have a +2 bonus to Strength. They are able to carry heavy loads and perform feats of great strength with ease.
  • Courage: Centaurs are known for their courage and their bravery, and they have a +2 bonus to Charisma. They are able to inspire and motivate their allies, and they are not afraid to face danger head-on.
    • Charge: If you move at least 30 feet straight toward a target and then hit it with a melee weapon attack on the same turn, you can immediately follow that attack with a bonus action, making one attack against the target with your hooves (1d4 + Strength Mod bludgeoning damage).
  • Tracking: Centaurs are skilled in tracking and hunting, and they have a +2 bonus to Wisdom. They are able to follow the trail of their enemies and track their prey with great accuracy.
  • Base Speed: 40ft
  • Size: 7-9ft; Medium
  • Languages: Common, Sylvan
