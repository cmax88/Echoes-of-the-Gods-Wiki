Races

Explore the various races that inhabit the world of Gaia!

Brontes


The Brontes are a race of human-sized cyclops, known for their immense strength and intimidating appearance. They are large creatures, standing at around eight to ten feet tall, with broad shoulders and a muscular build. Their most distinctive feature is their single eye, which is large and glowing, set in the center of their forehead.

Humans


The Humans of Ancient Greece are a diverse and resilient race, known for their adaptability and versatility. They are proud and ambitious people, driven by a desire for knowledge, adventure, and power.

Atlantians


The Atlantians are a powerful and ancient race that hails from the sunken city of Atlantis. They are a proud and regal people, known for their mastery of magic and their advanced technology.

Amazonians


The Amazonians are a proud and fierce race of warrior women who hail from the lush forests of the Amazon. They are known for their skill in battle and their unwavering loyalty to their tribe and their queen.

Dryads


The Dryads are a mystical race of tree-dwelling creatures who are deeply connected to the magic of nature. They are known for their beauty and their grace, and they are respected by all who know them.

Centaurs


The Centaurs are a proud and noble race of half-human, half-horse creatures who are known for their strength and their courage. They are highly respected by all who know them, and they are known for their wisdom and their skill in battle.

Panes


The Panes are a mysterious and reclusive race of half-man, half-goat creatures who are known for their cunning and their skill in magic. They are highly sought after by many adventurers, as they are known for their knowledge of the ancient and powerful magic of the world.

Dracaenae


The Dracaenae are a race of half-dragon, half-human creatures who are known for their ferocity and their power. They are feared by many, as they are known for their ability to breathe fire and to use their sharp claws and teeth to defend themselves.

Automatons


The Automatons are a race of artificial beings created in the forge of the god Hephaestus. They are made of metal and other materials, and they are designed to be the perfect servants and workers.

Acephalis


The Acephalis are a race of humanoids with the head of a horse instead of a human head. They are known for their speed, agility, and their strong sense of community. They are revered by many as symbols of freedom and independence, and they are respected for their strong connection to nature.

Panotiis


The Panotiis are a race of beings with large, floppy ears that cover their entire body. They are small and nimble creatures, standing at around three to four feet tall. Their large ears are their most distinctive feature, and they use them to sense their surroundings, communicate with each other, and even manipulate objects.
